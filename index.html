Dr. Raphael Stuhlmeier

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Faculty of Civil & Environmental Engineering at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, working on wave-power harvesting in the open ocean.
Prior to that I completed a PhD in mathematics at the University of Vienna, working on gravity water waves from the perspective of the incompressible Euler equations.
Broadly speaking, my interests include ocean surface waves, among them tsunami, stratified-density flows, and waves with vorticity (in particular Gerstner's wave).
