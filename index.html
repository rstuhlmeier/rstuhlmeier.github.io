Dr. Raphael Stuhlmeier

I am Lecturer in Applied Mathematics at the University of Plymouth, with interests in ocean surface waves. Prior to that I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Faculty of Civil & Environmental Engineering at Technion - Israel Institute of Technology. I completed a PhD in mathematics at the University of Vienna in 2014.
