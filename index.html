Dr. Raphael Stuhlmeier

I am Lecturer in Applied Mathematics at Plymouth University, with interests in ocean surface waves. Prior to that I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Faculty of Civil & Environmental Engineering at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology. I completed a PhD in mathematics at the University of Vienna in 2014. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Faculty of Civil & Environmental Engineering at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, working on wave-power harvesting in the open ocean.
