Water Waves - Mathematical Theory and Applications

A workshop to be held at the University of Plymouth, September 5-6, 2019, on theoretical and applied aspects of water waves.

If you are interested in attending, please register using this link. Registration is free, and suggestions for contributed talks (ca. 45') are welcome. If you are interested in giving a talk, please email the organizer Raphael Stuhlmeier.

Invited Speakers

  • Emilian Parau (UEA)
  • Raphael Stuhlmeier (Plymouth)
  • Alison Raby (Plymouth) (tentative)
  • Daniel Conley (Plymouth)(tentative)
  • Anna Kalogirou (Nottingham) (tentative)

Funded by a Celebrating New Appointments Grant from the London Mathematical Society.
