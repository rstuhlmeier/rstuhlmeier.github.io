Linear Algebra (and Complex Numbers)

During the fall semester 2018 I taught the first half of a one semester course in Linear Algebra & Complex Numbers for first year mathematics students at the University of Plymouth.

The material in this course was based largely on the book

  • Strang, Gilbert, Introduction to Linear Algebra, 5th edition.
Most of the material associated with the book is available through MIT OCW or the book's website. I did solve a selection of problems in some detail, and provide these here for teaching or learning.
