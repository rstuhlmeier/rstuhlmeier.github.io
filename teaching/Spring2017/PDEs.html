Partial Differential Equations Problem Class

During the spring semester 2017 I taught exercise classes in Partial Differential Equations for students of Chemical Engineering at Technion's International School. For students, more information can be found on the moodle.

Much of my material was based on the books

  • Strauss, Walter A. Partial differential equations. Vol. 92. New York: Wiley, 1992
  • Pinchover, Yehuda, and Jacob Rubinstein. An introduction to partial differential equations. Cambridge university press, 2005.

I was happy to be able to make use of some online material (especially from Jerry Kazdan), and some of my course material is shared below. If you use it and find any errors, please let me know!
