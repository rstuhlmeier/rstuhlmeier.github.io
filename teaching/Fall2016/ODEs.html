Ordinary Differential Equations - Problem Class

During the fall semester 2016 I taught exercise classes in Ordinary Differential Equations for students of Civil and Chemical Engineering at Technion's International School. Students may find all relevant material on the moodle.

The material in this course was based largely on the book

  • Boyce, William E., and Richard C. DiPrima. "Elementary Differential Equations and Boundary Value Problems." (2001).
I am always happy to find usable collections of problems, solutions, and other material online. Marcel Finan's website and Electronic Textbooks are a great source of problems. I hope that some of the material below might be useful to you, whether you are teaching or learning.
